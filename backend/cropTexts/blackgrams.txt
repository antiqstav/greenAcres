Black Gram
Black gram, or urad dal, is a protein-rich legume widely cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.

How to plant it:

Sow seeds directly into well-drained soil.
Ensure row spacing of 30-45 cm and seed spacing of 10-15 cm.
Best practices:

Use disease-resistant seed varieties.
Avoid water stagnation as it can harm roots.
Regularly monitor for pests like pod borers.
